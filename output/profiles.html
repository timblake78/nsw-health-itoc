Profiles defined as part of this Guide

The following profiles have been defined for this implementation guide.

Infrastructure Profiles

  • NSW Health iTOC MessageHeader - Message header for inclusion in message bundles

Administration Profiles

  • NSW Health iTOC Patient - A patient with a biological sex extension and additional mandatory elements
  • NSW Health iTOC Practitioner - An individual health practitioner requesting medications, or administering or witnessing administration
  • NSW Health iTOC Encounter - An encounter with a medication check undertaken extension

Medication Profiles

  • NSW Health iTOC Medication - A Medication for use in a NSW Health context
  • NSW Health iTOC MedicationStatement - A MedicationStatement with a diluent extension and mandatory status and basedOn
  • NSW Health iTOC MedicationAdministration - A MedicationAdministration with a diluent extension and mandatory prescription and performer
  • NSW Health iTOC Order - A medication order (request) with mandatory status and requester
  • NSW Health iTOC Medication List - A list of medications in a NSW Health context

Clinical Profiles

  • NSW Health iTOC Allergy Intolerance - An allergy or intolerance in a NSW Health context
  • NSW Health iTOC Allergy Intolerance List - An allergy or intolerance list in a NSW Health context

Profiles on Data Types

  • NSW Health iTOC Dosage - Dosage information in a NSW Health context
