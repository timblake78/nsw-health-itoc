1.0 StructureDefinition-nswhealthitocmedicationstatement

NSW Health iTOC Medication Statement

A medication statement, extended to include an optional reference to a diluent medication.

Usage Notes

To be completed.

Examples

Practitioner reports patient is taking Amlodipine 10 mg Tablet once per day in the morning (as part of Medication History)

Practitioner reports patient is taking Salbutamol 200 microg every 4 hours as needed (as part of Medication History)

Practitioner reports patient is taking a Pantoprazole tablet once per day in the morning (as part of Medication History), with Cerner and IMDSoft codes
